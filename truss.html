Truss

A convinient way to track and manage projects.

Once you are signed in with Truss you will be able to create and manage any projects you are a part of or, join any projects your friends have giving you the project code to join. To create a project, it's as simple as clicking on the plus in the bottom left corner of the screen and giving the project a name.

After you open a project you will be able to create and manage the current status of issue pertaining to said project. This can be easily doine by clicking on any of the category buttons located at the bottom of each issue. You also have the option to archive any old or irrelevant issues using the archive button in the top left of an issue. Creating issues and accessing archives can both be done with the buttons in the bottom left corner of the screen. If you wanted to message and chat live about a particular issue, you can use the chat icon in the top right corner of the issue to open its related chat

Now you are inside the chat, you will be able to view messages from and send messages to anyone who is also in the project!
